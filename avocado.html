Avocados are rich in oleic acid, a monounsa fat which helps lower cholesterol levels. The American Heart Association say that Source maintaining healthy cholesterol levels with healthful fats could reduce the risk of heart disease and stroke.

Like bananas, avocados are rich in potassium. They also contain lutein, an antioxidant which is important for healthy eyes and skin.
