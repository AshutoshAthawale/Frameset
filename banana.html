Bananas are well known for their high potassium content. A medium banana contains Source 422 mg of the adequate adult intake Source of 4,500 mg of potassium. Potassium helps the body control heart rate and blood pressure.

Bananas are also a good source of energy, with one banana containing 105 calories and 26.95 g of carbohydrate.

  2. 32 mg magnesium
  3. 6 mg calcium
